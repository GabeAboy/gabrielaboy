Four trigger types

Triggers that
don't pretend life is simple.

Combine any of the four. Add preconditions. Cancel the others when one fires.

01 · Time

Time.
RRULE-grade.

Every-N-days. Nth-weekday. Last business day. First & third Tuesday. The schedules cron can't.

02 · Location

Location.
Enter, exit, dwell.

Geofence per person. "Trash out, but only if it isn't already at the curb." Predicate, not just trigger.

03 · Weather

Weather.
Before the rain.

"Bring the cushions in if precip ≥ 40% within 6h." WeatherKit-backed, per home location.

04 · Chain

Chain.
One done, next fires.

Laundry done → 45m later, "move to dryer." On-complete and on-overdue. Real workflows, not isolated pings.

Say it once.
In English.

Type the rule the way you'd say it out loud. Claude parses. You confirm. Tandem schedules.
