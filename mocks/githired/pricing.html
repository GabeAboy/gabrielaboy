Questions before you start

Can I try Premium before I commit?
Yes, every paid plan starts with a 14-day trial. You won’t be charged until day 15, and you can cancel any time from Settings → Plan & billing.
What happens to my data if I downgrade?
Nothing is deleted. Tracked jobs above the Free limit move into a read-only archive, and gated features (AI drafting, integrations) lock until you upgrade again. Export is always available on every tier.
Is the browser extension a separate purchase?
No. The extension is part of Base and Premium, install it from the Chrome and Firefox stores and sign in with your account.
How does the AI billing work? Will I hit a usage wall?
Premium is unlimited for normal individual job-search use. We don’t meter drafts. If we detect automated or abusive usage we’ll reach out before throttling.
Do you offer student or career-coach pricing?
Students get 40% off Premium with a valid .edu address. Coaches and outplacement firms, reach out for team plans.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major cards via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover). Annual plans can also be paid by ACH or invoice.
