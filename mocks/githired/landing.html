Before you sign up

Questions worth answering up front.

Is it really free?
Yes. The Free tier doesn’t expire and we don’t ask for a card. You can track up to 10 active jobs, enough to feel out whether the workspace fits before you upgrade.
What’s the difference between Base and Premium?
Base unlocks unlimited jobs, Job posting breakdown, Skill match, the document editor, interview prep, and network & contacts. Premium adds AI resume tailoring, cover letters, and application-question answers, the drafting layer.
Does my data stay private?
Yes. We don’t train on your resume, we don’t share data with third parties, and you can export or delete everything anytime from Settings → Data.
Will the AI make things up?
It writes only from what’s in your profile. No hallucinated employers, no invented projects, no fake numbers. If a job posting wants something you don’t have, we flag the gap, we don’t fabricate the experience.
